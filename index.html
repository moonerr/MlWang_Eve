? Why young adults
As a semester project, we are supposed to build one prototype to demonstrate our concepts. Even though we found being stressful can occur to anyone, we still need to focus on a specific group to tailor the requirements and constraints to practical design work. We choose young adults, not only for they are facing much stress and also for it is more accessible for us to observe their pressure and talk with them about their emotions. And also, we will be more capable of understanding and sharing feelings with this group.

🌝 Intended Concept

My solution for future Mundane to this problem is to build a private space for the public. It should be accessible for everybody as destressing would become a natural needs for everyone in a crazy assumption of the future.
When someone is entering, this space begins to work. Users can set the brightness of the whole to suit them best. The entire area has a cylindrical shape with a soft floor and round wall.
They could stand, sit or lie down; with either posture, they could find their way to throw, squeeze, slap the ball-shaped bodies(planets) to generate multiple changes to this space. These changes include vibration, colours, surrounding sound. And with the interactive process, everyone could get their stress released by multi-sensory therapy.

How does the suround sound work?

Hardware

  1. - Force Sensitive Resistors (FSR) * 2

    2. FSR is Used for detecting the pressure applied by two hands. It converts the force to an analog voltage. Arduino converts the analog voltage into digital one, ranged from 0 to 1023. The harder users squeeze FSR, the bigger the number is.

  2. - Arduino Uno

    3. It is a basic set for makers, powered by a USB porter or any 5V power port.

  3. - Laptop(processing)

    4. Lap top is used for executing Processing code and give out sound with its two-channel speakers.

  4. - Vibrating Mini Motor Disc (VM) * 2

    5. VM is offering vibration feedback to users. Arduino could send analog voltages by 'analogWrite(PIN, VALUE)' as input to power the motors.
